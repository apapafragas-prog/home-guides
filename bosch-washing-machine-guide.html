Washing Machine Guide

Quick reference for guests
👕 How to Wash
1
Open the door and load your laundry. Don't overfill — leave some room for clothes to tumble freely.
2
Pull out the detergent drawer and add detergent to the middle compartment (marked II). One cap of the liquid detergent provided is enough.
3
Close the door firmly until you hear it click shut.
4
Press the Power button (left side of the panel). The display will light up.
5
Turn the programme dial to select your cycle (see recommendations below). The display shows the estimated time.
6
Press Start/Pause ▶. The door locks and the wash begins.
7
When finished, the display shows 0:00. Wait 1–2 minutes for the door to unlock, then remove your clothes.

⚡ Handy Features
⏩ Speed Perfect
Press the Speed Perfect button to cut cycle time by up to 65% — same clean, much faster.
🧦 Reload (add forgotten items)
Forgot a sock? Press Start/Pause, wait for the door to unlock, add the item, close, and press Start again.
💡 Important Tips
⚠️ Don't force the door
The door locks during the cycle. Wait 1–2 minutes after the cycle ends for it to unlock automatically.
💧 Detergent amount
Less is more! One cap of liquid detergent is enough. Too much creates excess foam and extends the rinse.
🧺 After washing
Please remove clothes promptly and leave the door slightly open to prevent mildew and odours.
🚫 Please don't
Don't wash shoes, heavy blankets, or items with zippers left open. Empty all pockets before loading.
🔧 Quick Troubleshooting
Machine won't start?
Make sure the door is firmly closed (push until it clicks). Check the Power button is on and that Start/Pause has been pressed.
Door won't open?
Wait 2–3 minutes after the cycle ends. If still locked, press Start/Pause once, wait, then try again.
Machine vibrates excessively?
The load may be unbalanced. Pause, open, redistribute the clothes evenly, and restart.
Error code on display?
Turn the machine off, wait 30 seconds, turn it back on and try again. If the error persists, please message your host.
