Washing Machine Guide

Quick reference for guests
👕 How to Wash
1
Open the door and load your laundry. Don't overfill — leave some room for clothes to move.
2
Add detergent to the left compartment of the drawer (marked II). Use the liquid detergent provided — one cap is enough.
3
Close the door firmly until you hear it click.
4
Press the Power button (top-left). The display will light up.
5
Turn the dial to select your cycle (see recommendations below).
6
Press Start/Pause. The door will lock and the wash begins.
7
When finished, the machine beeps. Wait 1–2 minutes for the door to unlock, then remove your clothes.

💡 Important Tips
⚠️ Don't force the door
The door locks during the cycle. Wait 1–2 minutes after the cycle ends for it to unlock automatically.
💧 Detergent amount
Less is more! One cap of liquid detergent is enough. Too much creates excess foam and longer rinse times.
🧺 After washing
Please remove clothes promptly and leave the door slightly open to prevent mildew.
🚫 Please don't
Don't wash shoes, heavy blankets, or sandy beach items. Shake sand off clothes before loading.
🔧 Quick Troubleshooting
Machine won't start?
Make sure the door is firmly closed (push it until it clicks). Check that Power is on.
Door won't open?
Wait 2–3 minutes after the cycle ends. If still locked, press Start/Pause once, wait, then try again.
Machine vibrates a lot?
The load may be unbalanced. Pause, open, redistribute the clothes evenly, and restart.
