Dishwasher Guide

Quick reference for guests
🍽️ How to Run a Wash
1
Scrape off large food remains from plates — no need to rinse them, just remove the big pieces.
2
Load the dishwasher (see loading tips below). Make sure nothing blocks the spray arms from spinning.
3
Add a dishwasher tablet to the detergent compartment on the inside of the door (flip open the cover, drop the tablet in, close).
4
Close the door firmly.
5
Press the Power button (left side, marked I/0). The display will light up.
6
Select a programme by pressing the programme buttons on the panel (see recommendations below).
7
Press Start (right side). The programme begins and the display shows the remaining time.
8
When finished, the display shows 0:00. Wait 15–30 minutes before unloading for best drying results.

📥 Loading Tips
Upper rack
Cups, glasses, mugs, small bowls, and plastic containers. Place upside down and tilted so water drains off.
Lower rack
Plates, large bowls, pots, pans, and baking trays. Face them towards the centre for best cleaning.
Cutlery basket
Mix spoons, forks, and knives with handles alternating up/down so they don't nest together. Knives with points down.
⚡ Handy Features
⏰ Delay timer
Press the delay button to set the dishwasher to start later (e.g., overnight). Each press adds hours.
½ Half Load
If you only have a few items, press the Half Load button before starting. It saves water and energy.
💡 Important Tips
⚠️ Water tap
Make sure the water supply tap (under the sink) is turned on. If the dishwasher shows a water error, check the tap first.
🚫 Not dishwasher safe
Don't put wooden boards, sharp knives, non-stick pans, crystal glasses, or aluminium items (moka pot) in the dishwasher.
🌀 Check the spray arms
Before starting, make sure no tall item (pot handle, ladle) blocks the spray arms from rotating freely.
💧 Unloading order
Always unload the bottom rack first — otherwise, water trapped in upside-down cups on the top rack will drip onto clean dishes below.
🔧 Quick Troubleshooting
Dishwasher won't start?
Check that the door is firmly closed, Power is on, and you pressed Start. If the display shows a tap symbol, the water supply is off — check under the sink.
Dishes still dirty?
Make sure dishes face the centre, nothing blocks the spray arms, and you're using a tablet (not hand dish soap!).
Dishes not drying?
This is normal for condensation drying — just leave the door slightly ajar for 15–30 minutes after the cycle ends. Plastic items always dry slower.
